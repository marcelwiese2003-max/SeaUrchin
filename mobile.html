FAQ

Good to know

What should I bring?
A swimsuit and towel. All diving and snorkelling equipment is provided at the center.
When do you operate?
Monday to Saturday, all year round. Open 08:00–16:00. Closed Sundays and January 1st.
Do I need a medical certificate?
Not legally required in Mauritius. If you have health concerns we recommend consulting your doctor before travelling.
At what age can children dive?
Children join playful water activities from age 8, Junior OWD from age 10, Rescue Diver from 16, and DivePro from 18.
What night dive price do you charge?
Night dives are €80, subject to demand, weather and tidal conditions.
Do you cater for non-swimmers?
Yes — the Discover Scuba Diving experience is open to all. Our instructors adapt to every comfort level.
How many people per group?
Maximum 6 divers per guide — always. We believe diving should be personal, not a convoy.
